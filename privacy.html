Privacy Policy

1 · Introduction

ShopLink Sync (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This document explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and TikTok Shop integrations.

2 · Information we collect

Merchant data (catalog items, inventory, order information) is accessed only after you authorize the ShopLink Sync app inside TikTok Shop.

Website analytics are collected in aggregate using cookies/ similar tech for performance and troubleshooting.

3 · How we use data

  • Sync products and inventory between your e-commerce catalog and TikTok Shop.
  • Provide usage statistics and health checks for your store connection.
  • Respond to support requests.

4 · Your choices

You may revoke ShopLink Sync access at any time from TikTok Shop’s “Authorized Apps” panel or by contacting support@shoplinksync.com.
