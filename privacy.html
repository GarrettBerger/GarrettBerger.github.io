Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2025

ShopLink Sync (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a product-data synchronization service for ecommerce sellers. This page explains how we collect, use and share information when you visit shoplinksync.com or authorize our application on TikTok Shop.

Information we collect

  • Shop credentials – OAuth tokens that you explicitly grant.
  • Catalog data – titles, descriptions, prices, images and inventory figures you store in TikTok Shop or other marketplaces.
  • Usage analytics – anonymous page-view statistics collected by Cloudflare analytics. No cookies are set.

How we use information

  • Import product details into our workflow engine (n8n) so you can edit or repost them.
  • Publish updated listings to TikTok Shop on your behalf.
  • Provide support and improve the service.

Sharing of information

We never sell your data. Tokens and catalog data are stored on servers operated by n8n Cloud and are shared only with TikTok Shop via its official API.

Data retention & deletion

You may revoke our app in TikTok Shop at any time; all related tokens and catalog data are automatically purged within 30 days.
